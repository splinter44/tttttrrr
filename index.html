Faculty of Cybersecurity, Computer
and Software Engineering

The Faculty of Cybersecurity of Computer and Software Engineering of the
National Aviation University was established in 2012 by the order of the
Rector of the University dated 30.11.2012 №307 / unit
in order to concentrate the processes related to training IT specialists
and conducting research in computer technology.

Faculty of Environmental Safety,
Engineering and Technology

The Faculty of Environmental Safety, Engineering and Technology of the
National Aviation University from 12.10.2018 № 466 / unit. on the basis of the
decision of the Academic Council of NAU from 28.09.2018 (protocol № 6)
through the reorganization of the Institute of Environmental Safety.
The origins of the faculty are connected with the faculty of airports, which was
founded in 1968. The foundation of the faculty was laid by the faculties:
fuels and lubricants, aviation ground equipment, individual departments.
